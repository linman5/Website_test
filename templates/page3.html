Projects
I have created a number of different environments, hand painted in virtual reality using Tilt Brush. Take a look at all of my projects to date, including client work, my own art practice, my degree show work and other experiments.
Ninety One
Investec Asset Management have rebranded to Ninety One. This immersive timeline pathway takes the viewer through the development of their brand and the cultural influences that made them who they are from their nascent beginnings til now.

Google Automation Summit
I performed live on stage at the Google Automation Summit for 150 guests, creating an environment that described the topics that were going to be spoken about at the event.






Google Finance Week
Entertaining Google's Finance Week attendees with Tilt Brush, I created two drawings as templates of the Google logos for guests to embellish as they wish, facillitating many of their first tries using VR.






Forlorn Park
My final project for my Degree Show, completing my Art & Design degree. It explores the theme of urban parks and their potential insight into the human - nature relationship.
